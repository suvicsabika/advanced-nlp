Example GET request URLs:

GET - Get every news (us, 20 news, everything):
http://localhost:5000/api/news

GET - Get technology news:
http://localhost:5000/api/news?category=technology

GET - 10 latest Health news from Germany:
http://localhost:5000/api/news?category=health&country=de&page_size=10

POST - Generate fake headlines
http://localhost:5000/api/news_with_headlines
